Flooring

Providing clean, smooth, durable and impervious levelled surface to the occupants of the house and the said surface is called Floor. The Floor should be hard, durable and sufficiently strong to withstand the loads coming over it.

Plumbing

Plasting

Plasting is construction or ornamentation done with plaster, such as a layer of plaster on an interior or exterior wall structure, or plaster decorative moldings on ceilings or walls.

Carpentery
